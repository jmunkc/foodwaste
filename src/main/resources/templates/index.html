Welcome

This site was created to mediate contact between stores with out-dated food and people who want to eat it

How does it work?

At the Sign Up page, stores can sign up as a provider. At the Find Food page, people can search their zip code for participating stores with available food.

If you want more information about food waste, visit the Info page.
